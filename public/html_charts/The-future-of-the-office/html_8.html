Comprehensive Gen AI Measurement Framework
Google Cloud's approach to evaluating AI implementation across five key dimensions
Model Quality
Precision, recall, F1 scores for bounded outputs
Model-based auto-raters for unbounded outputs
Coherence, fluency, and safety metrics
Groundedness and instruction-following
System Quality
Deployment: # of deployed models, time-to-deployment
Reliability: uptime 99.9%, error rate 0.1%
Throughput: request & token processing rates
Resource utilization: GPU/TPU usage efficiency
Business Operational
Automation rate of routine tasks
Time savings on key workflows
Error reduction in processes
Capacity reallocation to higher-value work
Adoption
User engagement rates
Feature utilization percentage
User satisfaction scores
Cross-department implementation
Business Value
Revenue impact (direct and indirect)
Cost reduction
ROI calculation (avg. $3.50-3.70 per $1 invested)
Competitive advantage metrics
Source: Google Cloud "Measuring gen AI success" framework (2024), IDC-Microsoft study (2025)
