Framework for Mental Health Support in Flexible Work Environments
Psychosocial Interventions
• Self-care training and resources
• Virtual social connection activities
• Work-life boundary training
• Peer support networks
• Family engagement resources
Reduces: Psychological distress, burnout, social isolation, work-family conflict
Organizational Interventions
• Enhanced communication protocols
• Workload management tools
• Flexible scheduling options
• Career development for remote workers
• Financial assistance programs
Reduces: Job insecurity, turnover intentions, communication gaps, career growth concerns
Environmental Interventions
• Home office setup assistance
• Ergonomic assessment tools
• Technology support resources
• Digital disconnection policies
• Neighborhood workspace options
Reduces: Physical discomfort, technology stress, boundary challenges, isolation
Source: Adapted from Zhang et al. (Johns Hopkins, 2022)
